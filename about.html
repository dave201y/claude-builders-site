Empowering Mavericks to Build the Future of AI

Claude Builders is Minnesota State University Mankato’s student-led hub for exploring, building, and innovating with Claude AI. We welcome all skill levels—no experience required, just curiosity and a passion for what’s next in artificial intelligence.

Join Us Learn More
🚀
Our Mission
To empower Mavericks to explore, learn, and create with AI through hands-on projects, workshops, and real-world challenges—preparing students for tomorrow’s AI-driven world.
🌟
Our Vision
To be the Midwest’s leading university hub for AI innovation, collaboration, and ethical leadership—open to all, driven by curiosity and community.

What We Do

Workshops & Projects
Hands-on sessions, hackathons, and real-world projects using Claude AI and other cutting-edge tools.
AI Exploration
Dive into the latest in AI, prompt engineering, and ethical technology—no prior experience needed.
Community & Networking
Connect with fellow Mavericks, industry mentors, and alumni to build your future in tech.
Career Prep
Interview prep, resume reviews, and guidance for AI-driven careers—by students, for students.

Why Claude AI?

State-of-the-Art
Claude is one of the world’s most advanced conversational AIs—perfect for learning, building, and innovating.
Ethical & Responsible
We focus on responsible, ethical AI development and use—preparing students to lead with integrity.
Open to All
No coding or AI background required—just curiosity and a willingness to learn.

Membership Benefits

Real-World Experience
Build your portfolio with real projects, leadership roles, and hands-on AI experience.
Inclusive Community
Join a welcoming, diverse group of Mavericks passionate about technology and the future.
Career Connections
Network with industry leaders, alumni, and peers to launch your AI career.
